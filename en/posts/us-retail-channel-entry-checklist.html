US Retail Channel Entry: 30-Day Checklist

Published: 2026-03-02

This checklist is designed for brands that want to run Shopify growth and US retail channel entry in parallel.

Week 1: Channel priority and documentation checklist

  1. Shortlist 2-3 priority channels based on category fit.
  2. Prepare company, product, and supply documents.
  3. Align barcode, packaging, and compliance assets.

Week 2: Application and review workflow

  1. Submit onboarding applications and build milestone tracker.
  2. Prepare channel-specific supplemental files.
  3. Define launch assortment and pricing structure.

Week 3: Launch preparation and first SKU wave

  1. Finalize listing structures and media assets.
  2. Validate inventory, lead-time, and return policy setup.
  3. Set first-month sales and replenishment targets.

Week 4: Operations review and expansion roadmap

  1. Review traffic, clicks, conversion, and order quality.
  2. Improve low-performing listings and pricing.
  3. Define next-channel expansion plan.
